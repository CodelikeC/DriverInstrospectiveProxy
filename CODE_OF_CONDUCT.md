# Code of Conduct — Driver Introspective Proxy

DIP is a space for engineers, researchers, and architects passionate about adaptive introspection, system reasoning, and ethical AI.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in technical debates.
- Welcome new contributors and document your thought process clearly.
- Focus discussions on architecture, not individuals.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Hostility, discrimination, or harassment.
- Intentionally publishing system exploit paths or unsafe driver hooks.
- Using DIP to develop introspection for surveillance or non-consensual analysis.

---

## Enforcement

Violations will be reviewed by maintainers and may result in:
- Temporary or permanent ban from contribution.
- Removal of associated content.

---

> “The boundary between introspection and intrusion must never be crossed.”
